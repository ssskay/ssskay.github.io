      Hi, I'm Sara Kay and welcome to my website!

I am also on the board of the Hawaii Gifted Association (HGA) and help other gifted students like myself pursue their passions without restrictions. Additionally, I am a Mensa Foundation Scholarship recipient and breifly managed the database for the local chapter in Hawaii.

I also operate a custom sticker and pop up card store and partner with various organizations on campus, such as Women in Computer Science (WiCS) and Christian Union (CU).
